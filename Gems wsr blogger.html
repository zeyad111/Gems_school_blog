Welcome to GEMS Westminster School Ras Al Khaimah.
"Education is like a lantern which lights your way in a dark alley”
- Sheikh Zayed Bin Sultan Al Nahyan GEMS Westminster School Ras Al Khaimah (WSR) is a values-driven school, providing an outstanding education through our four core values: Leading Through Innovation, Pursuing Excellence, Growing by Learning and Global Citizenship. These values are woven into all GEMS schools and are the essence of our DNA. They are not symbolic; we live and breathe them. At WSR, we are committed to providing a first-class education for students. WSR is a richly diverse and exciting international learning community. We have high aspirations for all our students and believe that education is the passport to their future. We provide quality education, reflected in our excellent record of nurturing confidence in our students and guiding them to exceed their potential. The key characteristics of WSR are high expectations, aspirations, excellence and a belief that all can succeed; contributing creativity, innovation and enterprise alongside tradition and heritage; developing young people who are confident, resilient, compassionate and global citizens. From KG1 through to Grade 12, the quality of our all-through school allows parents to rest assured that their children are being nurtured to become truly remarkable citizens, contributing to their communities with empathy, tolerance and wisdom. As WSR students, they will hold themselves accountable to our high standards and, when the time comes, graduate with an education that will open doors to higher education, employment and beyond. Our teachers are the key to your child’s educational success; we ensure they are supported and highly skilled to provide the standards that we expect. Every child is unique and special; we harness their strengths and develop them into well-rounded individuals. Our curriculum is creative and exciting, underpinned by the National Curriculum for England. We ensure all students are equipped with the skills, knowledge and experience needed to progress successfully through each grade and key stage. Our teachers are highly qualified, using globally recognised teaching techniques and universal learning strategies to maximise each student’s potential. Our motto is One Tribe, One Family, which reflects our belief that it takes a community to instil and reinforce excellence. Through close collaboration with our WSR families, from home to school, our students are raised to be kind, caring and bright, and to excel in academic subjects, creative arts and sports. WSR graduates exude confidence in their abilities, empowered with the knowledge that they are ready for the journey that is life. We want our students to become global explorers who are intellectual, articulate, thoughtful and empathetic, who are socially, ethically and environmentally responsible. GEMS Westminster School Ras Al Khaimah, opened in 2013, is part of the GEMS Education group, one of the largest private K-12 education providers in the world, where ‘We see genius in every child’. I look forward to working with our community to continue to build a successful educational legacy for our children today and the generations that follow. Ms Nicole Haynes Principal/CEO






About our school


GEMS Westminster School - Ras Al Khaimah WSR is a real community that combines tradition and innovation in teaching and learning, enabling pupils to be the best they can be in all aspects of school life. We offer the internationally recognised National Curriculum for England, and prepare students to take the International General Certificate of Secondary Education (IGCSE) course and A Level Examinations of the Universities of Cambridge and London (UK). At the present time, the school offers this curriculum from Early Years Foundation Stage to Grade 11, and in successive years we will continue to expand at a measured pace and introduce higher education programmes. The decision to send your child to WSR is inevitably a very personal one, but we believe that our school is the correct choice, an opinion we base around our academic success, with results continuing to go from strength-to-strength. Our website is full of information about our school and we hope you enjoy finding out more about us. We very much look forward to meeting you and providing your child with the high quality education that brings them the joy and success they deserve!

Our values



Integrity


Integrity encompasses the virtues of being trustworthy, pro-active and of having the courage and initiative necessary to change those things which should be changed.



Responsibility


Responsibility requires the acceptance that each of us is answerable for our actions and accepting the positive or negative outcome of our actions.



Respect



Respect involves treating others with courtesy, consideration and understanding. Respect embraces the right of teachers to teach and students to learn, within a caring and supportive environment



Honesty


Honesty is about speaking and acting truthfully, a commitment to upholding that which is right and is a way of life not just a behavior.



Tolerance


Tolerance requires a person to accept individual differences, accept ideas, behaviors and feelings that are different and oppose harassment or prejudice in any form.

Academic Excellence



Fairness and tolerance are the fabric of our ethos and students are well-mannered and respectful. These positive relationships promote effective learning conditions supported by creative and engaging teaching that stretches all abilities.

Over the past three years GEMS Education students have been accepted to 590 universities in 41 countries. Furthermore, our students have won over 700 regional and international awards. This is backed-up by the strength of our alumni community, with students continuing to benefit both professionally and socially from their time at school through university, in the workplace and beyond.





Our facilities


Innovative New Building and Facilities



WSR provides high quality, affordable education to students of all nationalities. We believe that everyone is equal but unique, and that all students must be able to utilise their skills and talents fully.

Our English curriculum school in Ras Al Khaimah features elegant, spacious and well-designed classrooms on an outstanding campus. The aesthetically modern architecture is well suited to creating the perfect creative blend that is so necessary for a multi-cultural setting that provides the hallmark of all GEMS schools – a quality education.

The outstanding facilities at WSR include an indoor sports hall, outdoor football pitch, basketball court, cricket nets, outdoor multi-games courts, music, art and science labs, ICT and electronics labs, library and indoor play areas. These allow staff to create a rich and beneficial environment for your children.
